Weekend Recipe: Homemade Sourdough Bread

This weekend I tried making sourdough bread from scratch. It turned out great! Here is my recipe.

Ingredients

  • 500g bread flour
  • 350ml water
  • 100g active sourdough starter
  • 10g salt

Instructions

  1. Mix flour and water. Let rest 30 minutes.
  2. Add starter and salt. Fold the dough several times over 4 hours.
  3. Shape and proof overnight in the fridge.
  4. Bake at 450°F in a Dutch oven for 45 minutes.

The crust was perfectly crispy and the crumb had great texture. Enjoy!
